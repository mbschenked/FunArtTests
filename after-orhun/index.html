●  after orhun   /   three browser studies

Three browser studies after orhun's terminal-native dynamic art.

Each piece picks one motion thread from Orhun Parmaksız's terminal-art catalog and restages it as a browser-native rendering primitive — written from scratch, no Rust, no WASM. Source threads: orhun/theattyr, orhun/ratty, orhun/ratatui-splash-screen. Web-bridge precedent: ratatui/ratzilla. Visual homage; unaffiliated.

Wave Console
Same plane and matrix-rain texture as B, with the Möbius twist removed; camera locked dead-on so the wave rolls toward the viewer.

B2 · not similar
Glyph Polyhedron
A spinnable flat-shaded dodecahedron with a glyph atlas mapped to its faces, lit by a single directional light from upper-right. Drag to rotate.
3D · drag
B3 · wildly different
Glyph Particle Cloud
5000 glyph particles in a slow galaxy rotation, additively blended. No plane, no surface — only points in volume.

●  iteration 2   /   variants on B3  ·  same similarity gradient  ·  one mouse-proximity mechanic each
B3-1 · most similar
Solar Flare
Same galaxy-rotation + lissajous recipe as B3, recolored to a warm flare palette. Cursor near the cloud, and nearby particles push outward on a damped spring before snapping back through center.
springs · interactive
B3-2 · not similar
Glyph Swarm
4000 particles drifting along a curl-like vector field instead of a disk rotation. Cursor near the swarm fires nearby particles outward with a brief size collapse; friction draws them back into the field.
explodes · interactive
B3-3 · wildly different
Sediment Field
Canvas 2D, no WebGL. 1200 floating glyphs in a sunset palette. Brush a glyph with the cursor and it staggers, falls under gravity, and accumulates as sediment along the bottom edge.
falls · interactive
